FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a bathroom remodel take? +
Most bathroom remodels take 7–12 days. A tub-to-shower conversion or walk-in shower build typically runs 5–8 days. A full gut renovation with new tile, plumbing, and electrical takes 10–14 days. We give you an exact schedule before work starts — and we stick to it.
Do I need to move out during the remodel? +
No. We contain the work area completely, clean up each evening, and restore access as fast as possible. If your home has only one bathroom, we'll prioritize getting it functional each night. Most clients stay home throughout the entire project.
What if you find mold or hidden water damage? +
It happens — Seattle homes, especially those built before 2000, often have hidden moisture behind old tile. We include a mold inspection in every estimate. If we find damage, we stop, show you exactly what we found, and give you a written price to fix it before continuing. No surprise additions at the end.
Do you handle permits? +
Yes. All necessary permits are included in your project price. We know Seattle's permit requirements inside out and handle all paperwork with the city. You don't need to do anything — we manage it from start to final inspection.
Do you offer financing? +
Yes. We work with financing partners offering 0% APR for qualified buyers on projects over $5,000. Ask about financing options during your free estimate — many of our clients use it to get the full remodel they want rather than cutting corners.
Are you licensed and insured in Washington State? +
Absolutely. We are fully licensed with the Washington State Department of Labor & Industries (L&I) and carry comprehensive general liability insurance. We can provide proof of insurance and our license number before any project begins.
Do you use subcontractors? +
No. Every project is completed by our own trained, background-checked employees. This gives us full control over quality and timeline — and means you deal with us directly, not a random third party who doesn't know your project's history.
