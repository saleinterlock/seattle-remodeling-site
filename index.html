FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a bathroom remodel take? ▼
Most bathroom remodels take 7–12 days. A simple cosmetic update (vanity, toilet, paint) can be done in 3–4 days. A full gut renovation with new tile, plumbing, and electrical typically takes 10–14 days.
Do you offer financing? ▼
Yes! We work with financing partners that offer 0% APR for qualified buyers on projects over $5,000. Ask about this during your free estimate and we'll walk you through options.
Are you licensed and insured in Washington State? ▼
Yes. We are fully licensed with the Washington State Department of Labor & Industries (L&I) and carry full general liability insurance. We can provide proof of insurance before any project begins.
Do you use subcontractors? ▼
No. Every project is completed by our own trained, background-checked employees. This gives us full control over quality and timeline — and means you deal with us, not a random third party.
What areas of Seattle do you serve? ▼
We serve all of Seattle and surrounding areas including Bellevue, Kirkland, Redmond, Renton, Shoreline, Burien, and more. Give us a call or fill out the form and we'll confirm your area.
